Glossaire

Welcome on the page "Glossary", find here definitions of complicated words in the articles

Anthropology :

The human science that studies the human being in all its aspects.

Excision :

Partial or total ablation of the clitoris and the labia minora, with or without ablation of the labia majora. This practice is considered a form of control of women's sexuality.

Feminism :

Feminism is fighting for gender equality.

Patriarchy :

Idea according to which the authority always goes to the man.

Protofeminism :

Concept that designates the fact that feminism would have appeared long before its official appearance.

Tagline:

A short, easily remembered phrase that a company uses in its advertisements
